Elder Care @ Home

What we provide?
LCAH brings to you a comprehensive healthcare solution for all ageing needs. Our elder care service is specially crafted in catering to individual healthcare needs. With our service, a dedicated Health Expert is assigned, who ensures all your healthcare needs are taken care of. Through this plan, we also assist you with services such as Doctor Visits, Diagnostic Services and Physiotherapy..
Benefits of Elder Care Service
  • A comprehensive care plan for elders, created by our expert Medical practitioners.
  • Dedicated Health Expert – Our Health Expert ensures end-to-end co-ordination of support required and is the single point of contact for all needs.
  • Online Health Records – Patients and Guardians have access to reports and progress online anytime, anywhere. Also, progress summary is sent via email at regular intervals.
