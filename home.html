Who we are?

Life care at home (LCAH) committed to providing a higher standard person-centered rigorous clinical approach for you and your loved one’s personal/medical care. We hire caregivers who exemplify compassion and empathy. Our Knowledgeable home care professionals strive to give you quality service and peace of mind. We match them with you based on your need and lifestyle, so you get a caregiver that’s right for you. We don’t send caregivers without training. So we bring in highly qualified doctors and experts in each caregiver’s (Nurse/Attendants/ Physiotherapist/Childcare assistant) training and supervision.

Our Vision

LCAH will strive to be the most people-centric, credible and comprehensive home healthcare solution provider in India.

Trust

We operate with integrity, transparency, and accountability in all our business relationships.

Our Mission

LCAH will fulfill the vision by creating a service delivery model with “people centricity” at the core of it. Delivering credible clinical outcomes, for every patient, every time. Evolving a scalable and self-sustaining business model.

Quality Care

We believe in the primacy of high-quality healthcare for effective treatment of conditions.
